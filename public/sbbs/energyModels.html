Energy Modeling

SBBS provides in-depth building energy optimization for both new construction and existing commercial and residential buildings.   We utilize numerous energy modeling programs, including but not limited: TMY Based Energy Modeling, Green Building Studio, Insight 360 and Revit Building MEP. These programs utilize computer automated drawings along with a description of the building layout, constructions,  operating schedules, conditioning systems (lighting, HVAC, etc.), and utility rates provided by the user, along with weather data, to perform an hourly simulation of the building and to estimate utility bills.


Geothermal Innovations

Stony Brook Building Science specializes in geothermal energy. Geothermal systems use less oil, coal and gas and decrease the emission of greenhouse gasses. Geothermal energy is becoming more prevalent due to their eco-friendly ability to lower energy costs.

Plant Optimization

SBBS uses controls to provide efficient operational management for any residence or commercial building. By closely monitoring MEP systems, evaluating the processes in place and taking the essential steps to assure that the plant has the equipment that best uses its raw materials, and that this equipment is being used in the most efficient manner. 

Net Zero

Our goal is to create the most efficient building models and systems that can sustain themselves. By building net zero energy models, we hope to help others decrease their carbon footprint and reduce utility costs as much as possible.

Tools

Stony Brook Building Science utilizes multiple software tools in order to ensure quality assurance within our models. Using these analytical tools, we can precisely quote utility savings, data analysis, and energy outputs. These programs include but are not limited to: TMY Based Energy Modeling, Energy Plus, Matlab, Green Building Studio, Insight 360, NREL SAM and Revit Building MEP.
