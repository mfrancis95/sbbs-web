About

Stony Brook Building Science, LLC. is headquartered in the LI High Technology Incubator and specializes in advanced energy modeling and the development of solutions that optimize the energy efficiency of commercial and residential buildings. SBBS uniquely combines the disciplines of Energy Modeling, Mechanical Systems design and Building Automation. Efficiency measures are modeled, designed, and optimally controlled.
